About Us

Find out more about our dental clinic and our identity.

Thanks to the trust of thousands of patients and the recognition of the most important entities in the field, today DentalHealth is the leading clinic in America.

History

In 1969, Dr. John Doe established a dental office in the district of Riverdale. His son, Dr. James Doe, joined him 10 years later. Together they started the project of implementing a dental care system different from those that existed at that time, incorporating the latest technological advances and the best quality service.

Such was the initial success that the same patients, who came from distant districts, motivated the opening of other offices. First in Midtown (1986), then in Uptown (1991), later in Downtown (1997), and in Westside (2002). The most recent locations are in Eastside, Northwood, and Southridge.

At the same time, the tradition of DentalHealth has continued. Dr. Jane Doe joined the team of more than 100 doctors, assistants, and administrators that made up the DentalHealth family a few years ago.

DentalHealth Pillars

Mission

To deliver exceptional dental care and superior service, ensuring the highest standards of patient satisfaction and trust.

Vision

To become the foremost dental care provider in the region, leveraging cutting-edge technology and a dedicated team of skilled professionals to promote optimal oral health and well-being.

Quality Policy

At DentalHealth, we strive to exceed patient expectations by fostering a culture of continuous improvement, advancing our professional expertise, and embracing the latest innovations in dental technology.

Values

DentalHealth upholds core values of Integrity, Compassion, Excellence, Innovation, and Respect. These principles are integral to our commitment to providing exceptional care and service.

Social Responsibility

DentalHealth is dedicated to supporting community welfare and environmental sustainability. We actively engage in initiatives that support local communities, protect vulnerable populations, and promote overall well-being.
