Emergency Services

Being prepared for dental emergencies is crucial. Knowing what to do when an accident happens can make the difference between losing or saving teeth.

Any injury to your teeth or gums can be serious. Ignoring dental pain can lead to more extensive and costly treatments. At any time, any day of the year, your dental health is protected. A DentalHealth doctor will attend to you in the event of an emergency at our 123 Main Street, Anytown, location.

Dental Plan

Our Dental Plan is a saving's plan for the dental care of you and your family. For just $10 per month, deducted from your credit card, you can enjoy preferential rates and certain treatments covered 100% from the second month, which can only be used annually.

  • This amount will be fixed and will not increase over time.
  • Family members can be added at $8 per person.

DentalHealth Card

The DentalHealth Card is the first dental protection card with preferential discounts for you and 6 additional members.

  • One-time annual cost: $35
  • Renewal cost: $25
  • Benefits include:
    • Free evaluations.
    • 24/7 dental emergencies (123 Main Street, Anytown).
    • 50% off on basic dentistry (cleaning and scaling).
    • 20% off on general, preventive, and X-rays (panoramic, periapical, and cephalometric).
    • 10% off on cosmetic dentistry, orthodontics, oral rehabilitation, and implants.

DentalHealth Kids

At DentalHealth, we prioritize children. Our DentalHealth Kids program focuses on caring for our patients from prenatal stages to high school graduation, ensuring they reach adulthood with healthy and straight teeth.

At all DentalHealth locations, we have dedicated spaces for children to enjoy their visit. They will be cared for by specialists in pediatric dentistry and orthodontics, ensuring their experience is as pleasant and painless as possible.
